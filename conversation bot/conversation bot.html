Conversation  Bot

This is a simple conversation bot still in development.

Things to say to it:

  1. hello or hi or you can capitalize the H
  2. How is the weather today? or How's the weather today?
  3. How are you today? or How is your day going?
  4. Tell me about you
  5. What are you doing? or Whatcha doing?
  6. Who made you? or Who were you made by?
  7. oof or you can capitalize the first O
  8. yeet
  9. What is your favorite ideology?
  10. What is the time?
  11. hecc or you can capitalize the H
  12. Tell me a joke
  13. bye, bye!, or you can capitalize the B
  14. Calculator

Please know that this is case sensitive. You will need to type in the exact case as the list above.
